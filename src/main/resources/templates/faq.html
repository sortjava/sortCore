Account Settings & Membership Plans

What is the difference between an individual membership and a family membership?

An individual membership provides Sort Switch Online service to the Sort Account that purchased the service. Individual memberships are available in 1-month, 3-month, or 1-year increments.

A family membership provides the Sort Switch Online service to everyone in the purchaser's family group (up to 8 people). Family memberships are available in 12-month increments only.

Can I change the members in my family membership?

Yes. Members added to the owner's family group will be included in the family membership plan for as long as they remain in the group.

Can Sort Accounts with different country settings share a family membership?

Yes. However, not all features are available in all countries.
